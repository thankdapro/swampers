Welcome to the Murklands

The Swampers are a proud nation of pioneers, fishermen, and frog-tamers who have carved out a home deep in the boggy heart of the server. Where others see mud, we see opportunity. Where others see darkness, we see witch huts to raid.

Our roots run as deep as the mangrove trees, and our walls are built from the swamp itself. Join us, and become part of something slimy — and great.
